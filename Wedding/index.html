HOFMANN RANCH

448 Private Rd 4742, Castroville, TX 78009

Gorgeous, natural ranch with a secret heart: spectacular events! 370 acres of scenic landscape & Texas charm, Hofmann Ranch by Wedgewood Weddings is only 25 minutes from downtown San Antonio. This ranch-style wedding venue combines vintage and classic with modern and bohemian to create a choice of event areas for every type of couple. Explore a choice of ceremony and reception areas like the tranquil patio looking out over the Medina River Valley, the Grand Meadow Pavilion, or our magnificent Grand Hall with double oak staircases & opulent fixtures.
